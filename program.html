Environmental Conference of the Humanities

Hosted by The Univeristy of Lethbridge, Environmental Conference of the Humanities (ECOTH) is an annual gathering of scholars interested in environmental humanities. The conference embraces the study of all aspects of the enviroment in the context of the humanities, including—but not limited to—eco-ciritism, Enviromental history, enviromental philosophy and ethics, enviromental anthropology, polotical ecology, posthumanism and animal studies, as well as ecocultural studies.
