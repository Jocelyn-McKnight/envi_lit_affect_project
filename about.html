Environmental Conference of the Humanities

About

History

The first Conference on Environmental Humanities was held in 2004. At first a biennial affair, by 2019 the conference had grown with the field and welcomed hundreds of participants across Canada. It became an annual event in 2022, and has become comparable with other established environmental humanities conferences such as ALECC and ESAC.

The Congress holds sessions of papers, poster sessions, and workshops. ECOTH invites emerging researchers, undergraduate students, graduate students, post doctoral students, and independent scholars to submit paper proposals to the Conference
