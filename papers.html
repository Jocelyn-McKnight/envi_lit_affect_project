Environmental Conference of the Humanities

Call for Papers

ECOTH is an open conference that welcomes proposals for papers and posters on all topics within the field of Environmental Humanities. We strongly encourage undergraduate and graduate students, postdoctoral scholars, and other emerging researchers to submit their abstracts. Submissions reporting on work in progress or pilot studies are also encouraged, as the conference provides an inclusive platform for sharing ideas, receiving feedback, and fostering academic growth.

Guidlines

Abstracts should follow the format illustrated in the provided example. Use Times New Roman, 12-point font, with 1.15 line spacing. Titles should be italicized and limited to a single line. Include your name, academic title, affiliation (if applicable), university, and email address, all positioned in the bottom-right corner of the page. Abstracts are restricted to 250 words in length not including references. An additional page may be used for graphs/figures if necessary.

A PDF of your abstract should be sent to ecoth@gmail.com no later than 23:59(MST) on Janurary 32, 2025.

Authors may submit at most one single authored abstract OR one co-authored abstract. Each speaker will have 15 minutes to present and the last 5 minutes are reserved for discussion.
